About the website

Now a day in India driver booking system is getting very popular and Most of the people want an ease of travelling using drivers. Instead of asking for auto rickshaw and taxis. Since there are lots of applications available for driver booking but they use centralized approach to Maintain data. "vehicle Share" Portal is a web-based technology that will manage the records of the driver and users. This application provides convenient and best service to customer (who wants to hire a driver) and driver (which wants to register) who easily register himself/herself. In "vehicle Share" Portal we use PHP and MySQL Database.
